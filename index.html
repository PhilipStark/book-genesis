The problem with AI writing

Every AI tool generates text. None of them tell you if it's worth reading.

WEAKNESS #1

Pacing collapses in the middle

AI writes each chapter as an isolated unit. It doesn't feel the accumulated weight of the narrative. Every test manuscript had a sagging middle.

WEAKNESS #2

Everything sounds like AI

We identified 20 specific patterns that make AI prose detectable. Pattern #11 (Explanatory Extension) appeared in every chapter of a 14-chapter test.

WEAKNESS #3

Relentlessly competent

AI characters always manage their emotions, notice the right details, think thematically. Real people have irrelevant thoughts during funerals.

THE FIX

Evaluation + Controlled Chaos

A scoring system that measures quality across 7 dimensions, plus a Chaos Engine that injects the human noise AI naturally avoids.
