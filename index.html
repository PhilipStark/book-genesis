The Problem

AI generates text.
Nobody checks if it's worth reading.

We tested AI manuscripts from every major model against 15 bestsellers across 8 genres. The failures were systematic and predictable.

Failure #1 — Pacing

The sagging middle is universal

AI writes each chapter as an isolated unit. It doesn't feel accumulated narrative weight. Every test manuscript collapsed between chapters 4–8.

Failure #2 — Voice

20 patterns make it detectable

Pattern #11 (Explanatory Extension) appeared in every chapter of a 14-chapter manuscript. Every simile gets unpacked with a redundant second sentence.

Failure #3 — Humanity

Relentlessly, boringly competent

AI characters always manage their emotions, notice the right details, think thematically. Real people have irrelevant thoughts during funerals.

The Fix

Evaluation + Controlled Chaos

A scoring system that quantifies quality across 7 dimensions, plus a Chaos Engine that injects the human noise AI naturally avoids.

Core Systems

Built different. By design.

☈

Chaos Engine

The only writing pipeline with an agent designed to break its own output. Injects irrelevant thoughts during important moments, failed emotional composure, unprompted memories, cognitive distortions. The things that make fictional people feel like actual people instead of narrative vehicles.

⚠

Anti-AI Detection

20 specific patterns that fingerprint AI prose. Pattern #11 (Explanatory Extension) is the most common. The system detects and eliminates them at the sentence level.

◉

Voice Fingerprint

Per-character voice DNA with differentiation matrix. No two characters sound alike. Tracks vocabulary, sentence rhythm, speech patterns, and tics across the entire manuscript.

◆

Entity Tracker

Persistent tracking of characters, locations, timelines, and knowledge states. Zero plot holes. Zero continuity errors. Every detail is verified across chapters.

★

5 Simulated Beta Readers

Including a deliberately hostile reader profile. Anti-inflation rules mean no dimension can jump more than +0.5 per revision without citing specific passages. The system is adversarial by default because when the same AI writes and evaluates, confirmation bias is inevitable.
