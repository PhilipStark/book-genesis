## 📤 How to Submit

1. Fork this repo
2. Add your book to the table above
3. Open a PR with title: `[Showcase] Your Book Title`

**Include:**
- Book title and genre
- Genesis Score (run `/genesis-score`)
- Your GitHub handle
- Word count
- Optional: excerpt or cover image
